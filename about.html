Our Story

AI Longevity Co., Ltd. was founded in 2026 by a team of pharmaceutical scientists, computational researchers, and international business leaders who share one conviction: the next generation of medicines will be discovered, not just by chemistry, but by chemistry plus intelligence.

Our founders bring together extensive experience from active pharmaceutical research programs, AI-driven discovery workflows, and global pharmaceutical commercialization. Together, we are building an integrated platform that connects discovery, developability, and translational decision-making, closing the gap that drives most late-stage failures in drug development.

Academic Heritage

Born from the Center of Excellence in Natural Products for Ageing and Chronic Diseases.

AI Longevity emerged from the research community at CE-NPACD, a Center of Excellence at the Faculty of Pharmaceutical Sciences, Chulalongkorn University, with active programs in natural product chemistry, pharmacology, nanoformulation, and AI-assisted drug discovery.

Our founding team brings together principal investigators, researchers, and international collaborators who have worked together for years on translational pharmaceutical innovation.
